Reliable

Open Sourced

The FISCO BCOS is collaboratively built and maintained by the open source community. The objective is to create a secure and controllable financial-grade blockchain platform that meets the requirements of the collaborative businesses.

Collaborative Business Models

We believe open consortium chain is the key for unlocking the potential of Collaborative Businss Model.

FISCO BCOS is an open consortium chain platform, built with an aim to facilitate parternetships and inspire collaboration amongst businesses. It greatly improves collaboration efficiencies, enhances user experiences and significantly lowers the costs and risks involved.

Use Cases

Since its launch, dozens of use cases have been implemented based on the platform.

Supply Chain

Supply chain consortium builds a perfect financial trust system between Banks, Anchors and Suppliers to achieve efficient and faster funding liquidity.

Judicial Services

Digital Escrow Consortium Chain stores digital evidences on the chain, which could be retrieved for verification when needed. Using blockchain technology, digital evidences are signed, encrypted and referenced to maintain their authenticity, authority, privacy and relevancy. This greatly simplifies the arbitration process.

Loyalty Points

Loyalty Networks consortium chain makes interchanging of points between different loyalty points system possible, without the need of third party agencies. Decentralization levels the playing field among parties. It enhances the value of the points and greatly simplifies the clearing process.

About Us

The Financial Blockchain Shenzhen Consortium (FISCO) is a nonprofit organization jointly established by multiple financial institutions and financial information service companies on May 31, 2016. The FISCO membership is open to financial institutions and financial information service companies that agree to abide by the Consortium constitution.

Join the FISCO open consortium, community with strong mutual trust and explore with us the numerous possibilities of blockchain!

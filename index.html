OUR VISION

Collaborative Business Models

FISCO BCOS is the building block of the Open Consortium Chain, which provides the platform that includes modules such as Identity, Data and Event.

The Open Consortium Chain allows participants outside the chain to interact with the chain, enabling the collaborative business model.

OUR CHARACTERISTICS

Design Concept

Welcoming for everyone

Well defined access control and support of multiple certificate authority (CA) can widen the spectrum of participants to join into the Public Consortium chain.

Ready for Regulators & Auditors

Inherent design such as observer node, Blockchain browser and real-time data access allows Regulator & Auditor to interact with data on the chain.

Low maintenance cost

One-click deployment to simplify the release process, Contract Naming Service to keep publishing Smart Contract in ease, Diagnostic Tools to help IT Support with complete view of administration.

Open Sourced

Source code are constantly reviewed by community and available to public to achieve transparency.

About Us

The Financial Blockchain Shenzhen Consortium (FISCO) is a nonprofit organization jointly established by multiple financial institutions and financial information service companies on May 31, 2016. The FISCO membership is open to financial institutions and financial information service companies that agree to abide by the Consortium constitution.
